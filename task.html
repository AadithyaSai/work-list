TASK TILL NOW

  • Task 1:Create GoogleApps@IEEE account

    GoogleApps@IEEE provides numerous opportunities to IEEE volunteers by rendering them a robust capability for messaging with access to a unique IEEE email address, Google Drive, calendar, and contact services. It offers great privacy and credibility to the user. The participants are expected to create an account in GoogleApps@IEEE and submit their IEEE mail-id along with the screenshot of the account via the Google Form provided.

  • Task 2:Create a LinkedIn profile and share your profile ID.

    Creating a LinkedIn profile is the most effective and fastest professional means of networking that offers daily opportunities. LinkedIn is the platform connecting new people with their professional designations. The participants are expected to create a LinkedIn profile and build a network with at least 20 connections. Afterwards, they must share the URL of their profile via Google Form.

  • Task 3:Make five connections in IEEE Collabratec

    IEEE Collabratec is an integrated online community where technology professionals can network, collaborate and create in one central hub. Sign-up is free for everyone and it offers a suite of productive tools with exclusive features for IEEE members. To redeem points for this task, members have to create an account in IEEE Collabratec and establish 5 connections on the same. The participants are expected to upload the screenshot containing proof of completion of the task via the Google Form provided.

  • Task 4:Make five connections in IEEE Collabratec

    IEEE is involved in the technical programme development of numerous events including conferences, virtual events, training, workshops, and job fairs. The participants are expected to register and attend any IEEE Conference or Webinar. Only events from March 2022 will be taken into consideration. Each participant will score 3 points for registering and 10 points for attending each event. The screenshot of the registration confirmation mail and one taken while attending the event in which the date and name of the event have to be mentioned and must be shared through the Google form given below. Source Link: ieee.org

  • Task 5:Present your volunteering certificates

    Volunteering is an opportunity to try something new. It can help you make friends, learn new skills, build confidence, advance your career and develop a sense of purpose. The participants are expected to present their volunteering certificates for which they have volunteered at an IEEE event. The copy of your certificates is to be shared in the google form provided. If you are currently volunteering for an event then upload the necessary proof. Events from March 2022 will only be considered.

  • Task 6:Join any IEEE online Communities

    Communities in IEEE Collabratec are large forums for sharing and discussing trending technology topics, IEEE Societies, or IEEE aligned entities. Participants are expected to join any of the 5 IEEE's online communities in collabratec and you will get the exclusive access to a global network of 250,000+ IEEE and Society members. submit the screenshot of your details along with the Google form provided.  

  • Task 7:Explore something new

    IEEE Xplore Library is a powerful resource tool for the discovery of scientific and technical content published by the IEEE. For an engineer, it is one of the best resources to keep up to date with technical information. In this task, the participants are required to visit the IEEE Xplore Library and read any of the IEEE papers published. Afterward, the members should submit a pdf file containing an abstract based on the content they have read.
